Who We Are

Trusted By Buyers, Sellers & Investors

Chwink Dhillon Estate Developer is one of the leading realtors in Pune, offering valuable and trustworthy consultancy in the real estate domain for over 35 years. Established in 1987, we are a registered firm and registered broker with the Estate Association of Pune and NAR — National Association of Realtors India.

Our work extends from Flats, Bungalows, Commercial Places, Warehouses, Factories, and Lands — both Rental and Outright. Our business dealings span most of the Multinational Companies operating in Pune.

We have been catering to the Pune and Mumbai market for over three decades, instrumental in setting up large offices, factories, industries, and property development projects for MNCs. We have also successfully handled relocation of top management professionals by providing residential properties matched to their requirements.

Whether you are a buyer, seller, landlord, or tenant — we welcome any inquiry. The better informed we both become, the better you can be served. Our goal is to make things happen for you.

Our Journey

Over Three Decades of Excellence

From a small consultancy on Bund Garden Road to a full-service real estate firm trusted by multinational corporations.

01

1987

Founded in Pune

Chwink Dhillons Real Estate Developers was established on Bund Garden Road, Pune — with a mission to bring honest, professional consultancy to buyers and sellers.

02

1990s

MNC Expansion Era

As Pune emerged as an automotive and IT hub, we became the go-to consultancy for setting up offices, factories, and accommodations for multinational companies entering the Pune market.

03

2000s

Builder Partnerships

We forged long-term partnerships with Pune's top developers — Panchshil, Vascon, Kolte Patil, Godrej Properties — expanding our residential and commercial portfolio significantly.

04

2010s

Industrial & Warehousing

Expanded into industrial leasing and warehousing — handling factory land, built-to-suit factories, and large warehousing for Korean and Indian MNCs across Pune's industrial corridors.

05

Today

35+ Years Strong

Today, Chwink Dhillons Real Estate Developers remains a Registered Firm with NAR India, serving buyers, sellers, landlords, tenants, and MNCs across Pune and Mumbai.
